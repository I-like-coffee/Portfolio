Challenges Faced
  1. Challenge: Non-Playable-Character [NPC] Behaviour
    Solution:
    Movement:
    For each job that requires NPCs, I placed nodes forming a rough path to assist in moving the NPCs. NPCs are spawned at the very first nodes' position, and with the help of the built-in "PathFinding" Service, multiple closer nodes are calculated between each "manual node" as the NPCs traverse to the next "manual node" through moving to each "calculated node".
    Humanization:
    To give NPCs a more human touch, I implemented Speed, Patience and Willingness-to-Tip factors.
    The Speed factor determines the speed at which the NPC moves at. The Patience factor determines the maximum time a NPC is willing to wait at the cashier for their order before just skipping to "Leaving Shop" phase and leave the shop. The Willingness-to-Tip factor determines the money at which clients would be awarded for serving the NPC customer.
